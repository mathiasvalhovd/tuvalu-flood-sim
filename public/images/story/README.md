# Story Images

Add your images to this directory. Each image corresponds to a chapter in the story.

## Required Images

Place the following images in this folder:

1. **01-paradise.jpg** - Opening scene: Sunrise over Tuvalu, turquoise waters, peaceful atoll
   - Suggested: Beautiful landscape shot of Tuvalu/Funafuti

2. **02-family.jpg** - The family and their home
   - Suggested: Traditional Tuvaluan home or family portrait

3. **03-flooding.jpg** - Rising waters, coastal flooding
   - Suggested: High tide flooding, storm surge, water encroachment

4. **04-impact.jpg** - Damage from sea level rise
   - Suggested: Salt-damaged crops, eroded coastline, infrastructure damage

5. **05-treaty.jpg** - Community discussing the Australia-Tuvalu treaty
   - Suggested: Community gathering, meeting, or official announcement

6. **06-departure.jpg** - Family preparing to leave, packing belongings
   - Suggested: Emotional farewell scene, packed bags, looking back at home

7. **07-hope.jpg** - New beginning, looking toward the future
   - Suggested: Hopeful image - new horizon, plane/boat departure, or arrival in new place

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended size**: 1920x1280px or larger (3:2 aspect ratio works well)
- **Orientation**: Landscape preferred
- **File size**: Optimize for web (under 500KB each if possible)

## Naming Convention

Use the exact filenames listed above, or update the image paths in `src/App.jsx` if you use different names.

## Fallback

If an image is missing, the app will show a colored placeholder until you add the real image.
